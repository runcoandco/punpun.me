Memory Mantra

Every day, I fuel my mind and body with care—because every choice I make strengthens my memory and my future.

Every meal I eat, every step I take brings me closer to a sharper, stronger me.

I cherish my loved ones, our laughter, our stories—the big moments, the small ones, and especially every single one as Bo.

I’m in this for the long game, because memories aren’t just meant to fade—they're meant to be lived, relived, and told with way too much detail.

So I walk this path with love, purpose, and just the right amount of stubbornness.

I am present. I remember. I thrive. And yes, I’ll eat the good stuff!
