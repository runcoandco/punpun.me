Memory Mantra

Every day, I fuel my mind and body with care—because every choice I make strengthens my memory and my future.

I cherish my loved ones, our laughter, our stories—big, small, and especially as Bo.

Memories aren't meant to fade; they're meant to be lived, relived, and told with way too much detail.

So, I walk this path with love, purpose, and just the right amount of stubbornness.

I am present. I remember. I thrive. And yes, I'll eat what benefits me!
